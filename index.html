Tirth Purohit Kaun Hote Hain?

Tirth Purohit Haridwar jaise pavitra tirth sthalon par peedhiyon se seva dete aa rahe brahman hote hain jo Pind Daan, Shraddh, Asthi Visarjan aur anya karm shastriya vidhi se karwate hain.

Tirth Purohit ka Mahatva

Haridwar ke Tirth Purohit peedhiyon purane vanshavali (genealogy) ke record rakhte hain jisme parivar, gotra aur purkhon ki jankari hoti hai.

Kyu Tirth Purohit se Milna Chahiye?

  • Sahi vidhi se Shraddh aur Pind Daan karwane ke liye
  • Family records update karwane ke liye
  • Dharmik margdarshan paane ke liye
  • Pitron ki atma shanti ke liye

Tirth Purohit Kya-Kya Seva Karte Hain?

  • Pind Daan, Shraddh, Tarpan
  • Asthi Visarjan
  • Ganga Aarti aur Vishesh Pooja
  • Anya sanskar (Naamkaran, Grah Pravesh, etc.)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Pind Daan kab karna chahiye?

A. Pitru Paksha, Amavasya aur Shraddh ke din Pind Daan karna shubh mana jata hai.

Q. Asthi Visarjan Haridwar me hi kyu kiya jata hai?

A. Haridwar moksh nagri hai. Maa Ganga me asthi visarjan karne se atma ko shanti aur sadgati milti hai.

Q. Ganga Aarti ka time kya hota hai?

A. Haridwar me Ganga Aarti roz shaam ko hoti hai. Aarti ka samay mausam ke hisaab se thoda badal sakta hai.

Q. Kya Ganga Aarti ke liye advance booking zaroori hai?

A. VIP darshan aur vishesh pooja ke liye advance booking karna behtar hota hai, khaaskar peak season me.

Q. Kya Tirth Purohit family records (vanshavali) rakhte hain?

A. Haan, Haridwar ke Tirth Purohit peedhiyon se family genealogy records rakhte aaye hain jisme gotra aur purkhon ki jankari hoti hai.

Q. Haridwar me Pind Daan karane ka kharcha kitna hota hai?

A. Pind Daan ka kharcha vidhi, samay aur seva ke prakaar par nirbhar karta hai. Puri jankari ke liye pehle se Purohit se sampark karna uchit hota hai.

Q. Kya Asthi Visarjan ke liye pehle se booking kar sakte hain?

A. Haan, bheed ke dino me suvidha ke liye Asthi Visarjan ki advance booking karna achha rehta hai.

Q. Kya ek hi din me Pind Daan aur Asthi Visarjan dono ho sakte hain?

A. Kuch paristhitiyon me Purohit ki salah se ek hi din me dono karm kiye ja sakte hain. Yeh tithi aur vidhi par nirbhar karta hai.

Q. Haridwar me Tirth Purohit kaise milte hain?

A. Haridwar me registered Tirth Purohit pehle se booking ke madhyam se ya sthaniya mandiron aur ghaaton par mil jaate hain.
