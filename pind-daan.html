Pind Daan kya hai?

Pind Daan ek vaidik karm hai jo apne pitron (purkhon) ki atma ki shanti ke liye kiya jata hai.

Pind Daan kyu karte hain?

Manyaata hai ki Pind Daan se pitron ko moksh milta hai aur vansh par unka aashirwad bana rehta hai.

Pind Daan kab karte hain?

Q. Pind Daan kya hota hai?

A. Pind Daan pitron (purkhon) ki atma shanti ke liye kiya jane wala vaidik karm hai.

Q. Pind Daan Haridwar me hi kyu karte hain?

A. Haridwar pavitra tirth sthalon me se ek hai. Yahan Pind Daan karne se pitron ko sadgati milti hai.

Q. Pind Daan kab karna chahiye?

A. Pitru Paksha, Amavasya aur Shraddh ke din Pind Daan karna shubh mana jata hai.

Q. Kya Pind Daan ke liye pehle se booking kar sakte hain?

A. Haan, peak season me Pind Daan ki advance booking karna behtar hota hai.

Q. Pind Daan me kaun-kaun se saman lagte hain?

A. Pind, til, jal, kush, phool aur anya samagri Purohit dwara vyavasthit ki jati hai.
